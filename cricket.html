Cricket

Cricket is more than just a sport—it's a global passion that unites millions. Played between two teams of eleven players, it’s a thrilling contest of strategy, skill, and endurance. From the thunderous crack of the bat to the roar of the crowd, every match is a story in motion. Cricket is played on lush green ovals with a central pitch where the action unfolds. Iconic stadiums like Lord’s in London, the Melbourne Cricket Ground, Eden Gardens in Kolkata, and the National Stadium in Karachi have witnessed historic moments and legendary performances. Whether it’s the fast-paced excitement of T20s or the timeless drama of Test matches, cricket offers something for every fan. Step in, explore the rules, the formats, the legends, and the magic that makes cricket a game like no other.
